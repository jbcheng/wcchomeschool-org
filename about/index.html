Who We Are

About the Conference

A gathering of Christian families committed to raising the next generation in faith, truth, and godly character.

"美西基督徒在家教育大會」的主要目的，在於推動、裝備、聯結基督徒家庭，以在家教育方式養育敬虔後裔，讓兒女們在真理、品格、學業與生活技能各方面得以精進發展，父母也在其中得著建立，以建造基督化家庭為目標，影響世代，榮神益人！"

This conference is to promote, equip, and connect Christian families to raise godly offspring through homeschooling. We desire to see children excel in truth, character, academics, and life skills as parents are also encouraged and transformed along the way. We also aim to build Christ-centered families to impact future generations and to glorify God.

Our Mission

The West Coast Christian Homeschool Conference exists to serve and strengthen the Chinese-American Christian homeschooling community across California and the broader West Coast. Each year we bring together experienced educators, pastors, and parents to share wisdom, encouragement, and practical tools.

Organized by HEAR Ministries

The conference is organized under HEAR Ministries, a Christian non-profit dedicated to equipping families. Donations to support the conference are made payable to HEAR Ministries and are tax-deductible.
